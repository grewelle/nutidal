Origins

Nutidal started with a simple belief: building is the only way to grow a company. Richard Grewelle began his career at the intersection of genetics and mathematics. He wasn't satisfied only learning, so he joined the biotech startup ecosystem as a scientist to help build a new DNA synthesis platform. He then took this expertise in quantitative biology and nucleotide chemistry and applied it to drug target discovery, building workflows and advanced datasets to integrate with modern AI capabilities. He works with founders and scientists to grow their own platforms and thrive in the startup ecosystem.
