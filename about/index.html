About Okeke LLC

We build software and help businesses grow. Every technology we recommend, we've used ourselves. Every strategy we suggest, we've tested on our own products.

Our Story

Okeke LLC started with a simple belief: the best way to learn is by building. We didn't want to be consultants who only advise — we wanted to be builders who ship real products to real users.

So we built apps. We put them in the App Store. We learned what works and what doesn't through direct experience — not theory, not case studies, but actual downloads, revenue, and user feedback.

Today, our portfolio includes 8 published applications spanning business tools, educational apps, and creative utilities. Each one taught us something new about technology, user experience, and what it takes to build software people actually want to use.

That hands-on experience is what we bring to our consulting work. When we recommend a technology, it's because we've used it. When we suggest a growth strategy, it's because we've tested it. That's the difference between theory and experience.
